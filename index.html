Christian DL

Photography and Videography


Hi! I’m Christian. I'm a photographer and videographer based in Seattle with over 10 years of experience in videography, photography, graphic and web design, marketing, and social media management. I picked up a camera and camcorder at a young age growing up in Vancouver, BC and filmed and photographed my elementary and high school team sports. The camera technology and the mediums of publishing have changed, but I've kept that same creative curiousity I had when I picked up my first camera.

In college I majored in economics and minored in business while I worked for the Washington State University Athletics department. As a freelance utility I trained a rotating staff of incoming students in striking a show and running stationary cameras and handhelds. I also gained experience as a red hat, parabolic microphone, spotter, and with talent stats. Filming and editing practices and games, and running replays and graphics for the control room helped balance the monotony of school work and kept my creativity rolling.

I've kept that same creative ambition going from behind my camera with my photography and with my video edits. I love to work with ambitious artists, brands, and individuals using outstanding creative photography, videography, cinematography, and engaging content.

My camera takes me from one adventure to the next and I know I'll be learning and growing because I enjoy every part of the creative learning process.

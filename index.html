Christian DL
Director of Photography

Hi! I’m Christian DL, an Internationally published Canadian Director
of Photography based in Seattle with over 10 years of experience in videography
photography, graphic & web design, marketing and social media management.


I work with ambitious artists, brands, and individuals to making them look professional,
to stand out above competittion through outstanding creative photography, videogaphy, and engaging content.
Together, we can develop the perfect imagery for your press, website, and social media growth.


In this Digital Era, the phrase "You are what you share" has become more and more
popular. When we talk about any business or individual, the first thing we do is check
their social media channels, website, and photography content. The truth is that if
you don’t have an outstanding visual presence, you’ll struggle to engage with your audience.
You won’t be able to grow or look professional in your industry. Let me help you with this!
Just contact me through the button below and I’ll get back to you right away!

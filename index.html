Hi! I’m Christian DL, a Canadian Director of Photography based in Seattle with over 10 years of experience in videography photography, graphic & web design, marketing, and social media management.


I work with ambitious artists, brands, and individuals making them look professional, to stand out above competition using outstanding creative photography, videography, and engaging content. Together, we can develop the perfect imagery for your press, website, and social media growth.


In this Digital Era, the phrase "You Are What You Share" has become more and more popular. When we talk about any business or individual, the first thing we do is check their social media content, websites, and photography. The truth is that if you don’t have an outstanding visual presence, you’ll struggle to engage with your audience. You won’t be able to grow or look professional in your industry. Let me help you with this! Just contact me through the button below and I’ll get back to you right away!

I help artists, brands, and individuals create amazing videos, photography, and social media content so they can stand out online!

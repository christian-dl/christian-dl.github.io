Color Correcting and Grading Video

Aspect Ratio; 1:1 | Color Science Process:
Becoming a professional video colorist, I’ve developed 3 steps to get to where we need to be. First is Intention: Learn what the the creative director or person in charge is looking for in their video. The business we’re in naturally involves human interaction and connecting one on one early in the process (whether bonding over a quick cup of coffee or an all out storyboarding session) ensures the greatest likelihood for success. Second is Obstacle: Now that we know what to do, how do we get there? And finally Resolution: Now that we’ve got a plan let’s start using the tools available and the Color Science theories to get there.
